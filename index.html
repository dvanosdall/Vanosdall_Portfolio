About Me

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Cartography & GIS with a focus on GIS Development at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. With over 20 years of experience as a software engineer in an agile work environment, I have refined my skills in developing robust and scalable solutions. My passion for geography and spatial analysis inspired me to pursue this degree, where I combine my technical expertise with advanced cartographic and geologic principles to create impactful and innovative projects.

This portfolio highlights my academic journey in GIS, showcasing work from both my Master’s program and undergraduate research. It reflects my commitment to applying technology to geographic data interpretation and the projects that have helped me develop my skills in cartography and GIS development.
